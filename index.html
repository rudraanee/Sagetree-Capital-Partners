Who We Are

We sit exclusively on your side of the table.

Built on principle. Grown with purpose.

Sagetree Capital Partners is a regulated external asset manager managing multi-family offices — a boutique platform that serves a deliberately limited number of families with the depth and continuity of a single-family office, and shared infrastructure and investment capability across families.

We do not manufacture products. Our role is to design and oversee your family's total investment architecture, coordinate best-in-class specialists, and ensure the agreed strategy is implemented consistently across banks, custodians and jurisdictions.

The team behind Sagetree brings more than twenty years of experience at global private banks — experience now applied on one side of the table only: yours.

"We cap the number of client relationships to maintain depth, responsiveness and true partnership."

How We Invest — Three Commitments

I

Capital Preservation First

Long-term wealth creation comes from a clear plan, disciplined risk management and avoiding permanent capital loss. Real returns after inflation and fees. Transparent, understandable strategies over opaque complexity. Diversification by asset class, geography, currency and manager style — never concentration, excessive leverage, or illiquidity without a premium.

II

Independent Risk Oversight

Our independence extends to our risk function. Portfolios are reviewed against risk parameters set with you — not sold against product targets set by a bank. Risk is measured for your family, and reported to your family.

III

Bespoke by Risk Appetite

Every mandate is designed around your specific risk tolerance, liquidity needs and generational horizon. No model portfolios. No house-view templates.

Security & Structure

Our Mandate as Your Investment Manager

The strongest protection we offer is structural. Sagetree operates under DIFC Category 3C authorisation — portfolio management and investment advisory for professional and qualified clients — and the mandate is built so that control of your assets never leaves your family.

Your assets. Your name.

Client assets remain in segregated accounts in the client's own name, at leading international custodian banks. Sagetree never holds your capital.

Limited power of attorney.

We operate under a limited power of attorney — management and viewing rights only. The authority you grant is defined, documented and revocable.

No withdrawal rights.

We cannot move funds out of your accounts for non-investment purposes. Not partially. Not temporarily. Not at all.

Dismiss us at any time.

You can end the mandate without moving a single asset — the custody relationship is with your bank, not with Sagetree. Independence works both ways.
