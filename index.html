The Founder

Meghna Shah

Founder & Managing Partner
"True wealth management begins where product-selling ends — with a genuine understanding of what a client wants their legacy to look like."

Meghna Shah brings over two decades of distinguished private banking and wealth management expertise, having served in senior leadership roles at some of the world's most reputable financial institutions — including EFG Private Bank, Bank of Singapore, Barclays, and HSBC Dubai.

Her deep fluency in investment banking, capital markets, and bespoke wealth structuring across the MENA region and global financial centres forms the intellectual foundation of Sagetree Capital Partners.

Sagetree is her vision for a firm that places the client's long-term prosperity — not short-term product cycles — at the very heart of every engagement. Authorised and regulated by the DFSA under DIFC Category 3C, the firm operates with the full rigour of a globally benchmarked compliance framework.

Our Philosophy

The Sagetree approach to lasting wealth.

We believe that great wealth management is as much about relationships and values as it is about returns. Our approach is built on three principles.

◎
Clarity Over Complexity

We distil complex financial landscapes into clear, actionable strategies. Our clients always understand what we are doing and why — there are no black boxes, no hidden agendas, only transparent partnership.

⬡
Conviction-Driven

We do not follow markets blindly. Our recommendations are grounded in rigorous research, deep institutional knowledge, and the courage to take a considered position when conviction warrants it.

◈
Legacy as the Benchmark

Short-term performance is one metric among many. We design for generational continuity — ensuring that the wealth we steward outlasts the market cycles we navigate.
