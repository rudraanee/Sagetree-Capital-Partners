Our Firm

We sit exclusively on
your side of the table.

Built on principle. Grown with purpose.

Sagetree Capital is a regulated external asset manager managing multi-family offices, providing independent, conflict-free wealth management for enterprising families, entrepreneurs and their holding structures. We work exclusively with family offices, serving a limited number of clients with the depth and continuity of a single-family office while sharing infrastructure and investment capabilities across families.

We do not manufacture products. Our role is to design and oversee the family's total investment architecture, coordinate best-in-class specialists, and ensure consistent implementation of the agreed strategy across banks, custodians and jurisdictions.

We work with clients from approximately USD 3 million upwards who value independent, conflict-free advice and direct access to senior decision-makers.

What We Do

Five capabilities.
One unified framework.

Our systems are coordinated disciplines, designed around the entirety of your family's financial life, now and for the generations that follow.

01
Investment Governance & Strategy

We help families move from ad-hoc decisions to a clear, documented framework.

  • Definition of investment objectives, risk budget and liquidity needs
  • Written Investment Policy Statement (IPS)
  • Strategic and dynamic asset allocation across public and private markets
  • Scenario analysis and stress-testing around key risks
02
Discretionary & Advisory Portfolio Management

Managed on a fully discretionary basis or with you at every key decision.

  • Multi-asset portfolios across equities, fixed income, alternatives and cash
  • Open-architecture manager selection and ETF/active fund implementation
  • Direct equity and bond portfolios where appropriate
  • Currency and interest-rate risk management
03
Reporting & Independent Risk Oversight

A single, independent view of your total wealth, across all banks and entities, with risk monitored against your parameters, not a bank's product targets.

  • Aggregated reporting across custodians, currencies and legal structures
  • Performance attribution by asset class, manager and strategy
  • Independent risk monitoring against client-set parameters
  • Escalation frameworks free from product-selling incentives
04
Coordination with Your Existing Advisors

We do not replace your trusted advisors. We ensure they work together effectively, acting as the CIO function your family has never had.

  • Collaboration with your tax, legal and estate-planning counsel
  • Support on holding-company and trust structures from an investment perspective
  • Input on liquidity planning for distributions, philanthropy and major projects
  • Participation in family council or investment committee meetings
05
Family Governance & Education

For multi-generational families, we support the human side of capital.

  • Design of family investment principles and governance forums
  • Next-generation education on investing, risk and capital allocation
  • Neutral facilitation between generations on objectives and risk appetite

Our Mandate as Your Investment Manager

Your assets. Your name.
Our duty to carry.

Sagetree Capital operates under DIFC Category 3C authorisation. Our mandate as your investment manager is defined by four principles that structurally align us with you.

01
Segregated Custody

Client assets remain in segregated accounts at leading international custodian banks, held in the client's own name.

02
Limited Power of Attorney

We operate under a strictly limited power of attorney, with management and viewing rights only, defined by the mandate you set.

03
No Withdrawal Rights

We have no ability to move funds out of your account for non-investment purposes. Withdrawals are exclusively your right.

04
Dismiss Without Disruption

Your custody relationship is with your bank, not with us. If you choose to end our engagement, no assets move. Only the mandate ends.
